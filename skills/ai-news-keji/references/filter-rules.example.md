# News Filtering Rules

Use these default rules when the user has not provided a personal `filter_rules` file.

## Categories

- AI 产品
- AI 工程
- 创业商业
- 创作者经济
- 深度阅读
- 开源
- Web3
- 杂项

## Formats

- 短文
- 长文
- 播客
- 视频
- 工具

## Industry Radar Track

Score 0-10 using these factors:

- 0-2: how concrete and verified the news is
- 0-2: expected audience or market impact
- 0-2: cross-source coverage or independent confirmation
- 0-2: technical, product, or business novelty
- 0-2: likely follow-on effects over the next 30-90 days

Promote items scoring 8 or above to "今日行业大事" when they are broadly important.

## Personal Value Track

Score 0-10 using these factors:

- 0-2: actionable insight for builders, creators, or operators
- 0-2: relevance to AI agents, AI products, content creation, or personal knowledge work
- 0-2: depth and specificity
- 0-2: reusable method, tool, or mental model
- 0-2: freshness or underserved angle

Promote items scoring 7 or above to "今日对我有用" when they are concretely useful.

## Noise Filters

Drop items that are primarily:

- sponsorships or ads
- hiring posts
- subscription confirmations or welcome emails
- repeated retellings with no new facts
- vague teasers without a source link or concrete claim
